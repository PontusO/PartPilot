Multiplier that turns a component's material cost (what we pay the supplier) into its loaded internal cost — covering handling, storage, warehousing, procurement, etc. e.g. 1.30 = material + 30 %. This is not profit; components are never sold, only consumed to build assemblies. A part's own overhead factor (on its edit page) wins over this default.

Our profit margin, applied once at the sale: a finished product's customer price = its loaded build cost × this margin. e.g. 1.30 = a 30 % manufacturing margin. An assembly's own manufacturing margin (on its edit page) wins over this default. Flows through to customer orders, despatch and invoices.
