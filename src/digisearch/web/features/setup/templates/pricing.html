Multiplier applied to a part's cost to derive its customer sell price when the part has no explicit sell tiers and no per-part markup override. For example 1.30 means cost + 30 %. Used by the "Generate sell tiers from cost × markup" button on a part, the assembly BOM sell export, and the automatic price on a customer-order line. A part's own markup (on its edit page) always wins over this default.
