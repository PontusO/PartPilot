Multiplier that turns a component's material cost (what we pay the supplier) into its loaded internal cost — covering handling, storage, warehousing, procurement, etc. e.g. 1.30 = material + 30 %. This is not profit; components are never sold, only consumed to build assemblies. A part's own overhead factor (on its edit page) wins over this default. The loaded cost is what the customer is charged for the parts; production cost and profit margin are priced outside PartPilot (a per-product 97- part).
